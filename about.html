About the Creator

A journey built in public from the beginning.

CoderAnimeMe is not just a name — it is a long-term identity for building, learning, experimenting, and improving through code.

Who is CoderAnimeMe?

CoderAnimeMe is a growing programming creator identity focused on learning deeply, building honestly, and documenting progress publicly. The goal is not to pretend to be at the end of the journey, but to show the real path of growth.

Starting with Python and creative coding ideas, this website and channel are designed to evolve step by step into a richer body of work.

Current direction

The immediate focus is on fundamentals, experimentation, and building confidence through small meaningful projects.

Philosophy

Why build in public?

Public learning creates accountability, progress visibility, and stronger long-term motivation.

Learn honestly

Focus on true understanding instead of pretending to already know everything.

Build consistently

Small projects done regularly can become a strong body of work over time.

Share the process

Projects become more valuable when they are explained, documented, and connected to a real journey.

This is a beginning, not a finished identity.

The strength of CoderAnimeMe will come from progress over time — more projects, better explanations, stronger design, and more depth in both programming and communication.
